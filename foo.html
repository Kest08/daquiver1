UG Computer Science Department

ABOUT US

The Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana is renowned for its extensive offerings and contributions to Ghana's technical progress. The department gives students a strong foundation in computing principles, software development, data analysis, and other related fields by offering undergraduate and graduate degrees in computer science. Academics frequently work on projects in a variety of fields, including computer networks, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and database systems. These research projects provide a substantial contribution to the field of computer science's theoretical understanding as well as its practical applications. Through projects, internships, and partnerships with business partners, the department also places a strong emphasis on practical experience, which helps students apply their knowledge in real-world situations.
