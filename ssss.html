Student Resources

Sakai

  • Logging In
    • Access the Sakai Platform: Visit the URL provided by University Of Ghana to access the Sakai platform. Login Credentials: Use the credentials provided by University Of Ghana (username and password) to log in. Navigating Sakai: Dashboard:
  • Upon logging in
    • you will likely land on your dashboard. This typically displays courses, announcements, and other relevant information. Course Sites: Look for tabs, menus, or buttons to access your course sites. These could be listed as individual courses or grouped by semester or year. Course Materials: Once in a course site, you will find different sections for announcements, syllabus, assignments, readings, discussions, etc. Explore these sections to find the materials provided by your instructors.
  • Using Course Tools
    • Assignments: Instructors may post assignments here. You can view instructions, submit your work, and check for feedback. Discussions: Engage in online discussions with classmates and instructors. You can post and reply to topics, ask questions, and share resources. Resources: Access course materials like lecture slides, documents, videos, etc., uploaded by your instructors. Grades: Check your grades and feedback provided by instructors for assignments, quizzes, or exams.
  • Tips for Effective Use
    • Regular Check-ins: Visit Sakai frequently to stay updated on announcements, assignments, and discussions. Participate Actively: Engage in discussions, submit assignments on time, and actively participate in course activities. Ask for Help: If you are unsure about using specific features or encounter technical issues, reach out to your instructors or Sakai support for assistance
